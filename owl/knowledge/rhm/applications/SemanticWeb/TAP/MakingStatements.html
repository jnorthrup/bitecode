Making Statements with MKE + TAP

Making Statements with MKE + TAP

  • MKR to RDF shell script

    • # KEHOME/bin/mkr2rdf # Aug/16/2003 # convert MKR statements to RDF statements # usage: mkr2rdf mkrfile rdffile . tap.env export KBFILE="$PWD/$2" $ ke <<EOF let kbmode=tap; do open tap to $2 done; do read from "$1" done; exit; EOF

  • example MKR input to TAP

    • . tap.env $ ke ke$ let kbmode=tap; ke$ do open tap od $KEHOME/data to kbfile done; ke$ # KEHOME/data/DickMcCullough.html ke$ # Aug/16/2003 ke$ ke$ # MKR input for DickMcCullough.rdf ke$ ke$ DickMcCullough isu Person; ke$ DickMcCullough has ke> label = Richard H. McCullough, ke> hasGender = male, ke> hasResidence = PioneerCA; ke$ ke$ PioneerCA isu UnitedStatesCity; ke$ PioneerCA has ke> hasZipCode = 95666, ke> locatedIn = CaliforniaState; ke$ exit; $
