Expense Records

Expense Records

The files in the KEHOME/expense directory provide a simple record of expenses and yearly summaries by expense category. Each expense is assigned a unique id number which is used for recording attributes (see KEHOME/expense/expense.rel). The following attributes are recorded in the r_expense relation:

  • date
  • cost
  • receipt
  • check number
  • payee
  • category (make up your own)
  • note (any details)

To produce yearly summaries, enter
      ke summary.ku
The results are in the summary.out file.
